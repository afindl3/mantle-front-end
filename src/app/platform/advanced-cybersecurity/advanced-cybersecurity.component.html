Mantle has identified cybersecurity as a key area that benefits from using more than just blockchain. Through innovations across blockchain, cryptography and cross-cloud storage Mantle offers clients a secure way to share information from their valuable datasets.

SmartHashes - Digital Fingerprints Packed with Insights.

SmartHashes are able to capture significantly greater amounts of metadata compared to standard hashing methods. As a direct result, businesses are able to share important information and business insights without exposing the valuable data beneath.
